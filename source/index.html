Grace

For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works, so that no one can boast. ~Ephesians 2:8-9

We are a brand new church that is passionate about God's Word. Therefore, we believe that God has given us his greatest gift: through Jesus' death on the cross, all our sins have been removed. For this reason we proudly bear the name "Grace." This is the beautiful biblical word that God uses to describe the free gift he has given us in his son and our Lord, Jesus Christ.
